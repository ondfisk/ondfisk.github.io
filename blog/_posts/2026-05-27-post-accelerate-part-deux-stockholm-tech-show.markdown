Talk given at [*Stockholm Tech Show 2026*](https://stockholmtechshow.se/talarprogram-2026/) -- in Stockholm May 27, 2026.

Reflecting on decades of experience working with the largest enterprises around Europe I try to give you my take on why we fail at changing anything in the enterprise and give you my take on what to do about it.

<!--more-->

In order to change anything we need to rid ourselves of

## The kryptonite of change:

- Talking about change
- Planning change
- Meetings about change
- Status reports about change
- Reviews about change
- Documentation about change
- Seminars about change
- Post-it®s about change
- Everything which is about change but not the actual change

Then realise that the `powerOf(forgiveness) > powwerOf(permission)` and actually _do_ the change.
